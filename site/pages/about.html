Howdy! My name is Don-Vincent Snell and I've been a digital artist for 12 years.

I started with the creation of Iterative Flame Fractals before expanding into 3D modeling, animation, and video work. I have performed many duties as an artist, from video editing and web design to hardware consultation. I also dabble in game design and game writing as well as the occasional fiction piece.

I really enjoy playing games of all varieties and design content for various tabletop RPGs. Movies and their casual analysis is also a fun hobby. I also have a fascination with technology and space. I used to be a Library Director and have been a freelance artist for about 9 years. If you'd like to hire me or know more about me, don't hesitate to reach out.

Thanks for visiting my site and I hope you enjoy your stay!
